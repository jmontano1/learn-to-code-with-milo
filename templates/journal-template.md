## Notes

How to use this template: copy this file to `marketing/journal/week-NN.md` (for
example `week-01.md`), replace every front-matter line above with your real
answers, and keep each one to a single line. The front-matter block must stay
exactly in that shape and must be the very first thing in the file — MILO reads
it automatically, so the block IS your weekly report. Nobody will chase you for
status; this file is how the team knows.

Below the block, this Notes section is yours: write as much or as little as you
like, in your own words. What you tried, what surprised you, what you would do
differently. During launch week (lesson 23), add a short note under a `### Day N`
heading for each day you work — what you shipped, what happened, what you'll do
differently tomorrow. Commit at the end of each day with a clear message, e.g.
`journal: week 02 day 3 — first post published`. Delete these instructions in
your copy.
